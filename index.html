About Zilk

Zilk was founded by Carter King and Joseph Lecinski, both currently attending Utah State University in Logan, Utah, who wanted to make it easier for students to discover real, local restaurant deals.

As students themselves, they saw how difficult it was to keep track of discounts, promotions, and student-friendly pricing across town. Zilk was built to solve that problem — putting verified local deals in one simple app.

Our goal is to support local restaurants while helping students save money, all through a platform designed by students, for students.
